SAMPLE REGULATION EXCERPT — Data Protection (Illustrative)

Article 5 — Principles
1. Personal data shall be processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner.
2. Data minimization: collect only what is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary.
3. Storage limitation: retain personal data no longer than necessary for the purposes.

Article 12 — Transparency
Controllers shall provide concise, transparent, intelligible information about processing in plain language.

Article 15 — Access
Data subjects have the right to obtain confirmation of processing and a copy of personal data undergoing processing within 30 days.

Article 32 — Security
Controllers shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including pseudonymization, encryption, and regular testing of effectiveness.

Article 33 — Breach notification
Where feasible, the supervisory authority shall be notified within 72 hours of becoming aware of a personal data breach.

Article 44 — Transfers
Transfers of personal data to third countries shall take place only with appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.
